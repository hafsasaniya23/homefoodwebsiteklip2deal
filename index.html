About Klip2Deal HomeFood

Our Story

Klip2Deal HomeFood was born from a simple idea - to share the authentic flavors of Hyderabadi home cooking with our community. What started as a small kitchen experiment in Saidabad has grown into a beloved meal service, bringing ghar ka khana to homes across Hyderabad.

Our chef, with over 25 years of culinary experience, prepares each dish with the same care and attention as she would for her own family. We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

From our family to yours, we promise meals made with love, hygiene, and the true essence of Hyderabadi hospitality.
