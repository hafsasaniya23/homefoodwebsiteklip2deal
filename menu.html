Our Story

Meet Chef Ayesha

Born and raised in the bustling streets of Hyderabad, Chef Ayesha learned the art of Hyderabadi cooking from her grandmother. With over 20 years of experience, she brings authentic flavors and traditional techniques to every dish.

"My goal is simple - to share the warmth and love of homemade Hyderabadi food with busy families, working professionals, and anyone who misses the taste of ghar ka khana."
