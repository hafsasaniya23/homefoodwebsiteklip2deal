About Klip2Deal Homefood

Founded by a passionate home chef with decades of experience in traditional Hyderabadi cooking, Klip2Deal Homefood brings authentic flavors straight from our kitchen to your dining table.

Our mission is simple: to provide fresh, hygienic, and delicious home-cooked meals that remind you of the comfort and love of homemade food.

Every dish is prepared with the finest ingredients, traditional recipes passed down through generations, and most importantly - lots of love!

Our Promise:

  • 100% homemade taste with authentic flavors

  • Hygienic preparation and packaging
  • On-time delivery within Saidabad and surrounding areas (25 km radius)
