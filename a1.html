About Klip2Deal Homefood

Klip2Deal Homefood was born out of a passion for authentic Hyderabadi homecooked meals. Our founder, with decades of experience in traditional Hyderabadi cooking, wanted to share these cherished family recipes with the community.

We believe in cooking with the same love and care as you would at home, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional methods to bring out the true flavors of Hyderabad.

Whether you're craving comfort food, need healthy meal solutions, or want to experience authentic Hyderabadi cuisine, we've got you covered.
